## Hi there 👋
## I'm Pascal

A Data Analyst & BI Developer with 5+ years in analytics consulting. I've worked with organisations like USAID, the Gates Foundation, and the World Bank, building dashboards, data pipelines, and decision-support tools across Africa and beyond. I've also worked with a lot of businesses in the public and private sector - including government departments, development banks, other financial institutions, and corporate businesses. I'm currently based in Barcelona, Spain, where I completed an MSc in Business Analytics & Data Science (June 2026).

I care about turning (often complex) data into clear, actionable insights accessible to the people who need it.
